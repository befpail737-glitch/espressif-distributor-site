ESP32-H2

Ultra-Low Power RISC-V MCU for Thread, Zigbee & Bluetooth 5.2

Home / Products / ESP32-H2

Product Overview

The ESP32-H2 is an ultra-low-power, RISC-V-based MCU specifically designed for Thread, Zigbee 3.0, and Bluetooth 5.2 connectivity. It's the ideal choice for battery-powered IoT devices that require long-range mesh networking capabilities and years of battery life.

32-bit RISC-V Single Core up to 96 MHz
Thread/Zigbee 3.0 + Bluetooth 5.2
Ultra-Low Power Design
Battery-Powered Optimized
