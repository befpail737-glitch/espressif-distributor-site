ESP32-C6 Gains Matter 1.3 Certification

Revolutionary breakthrough in smart home interoperability standards

Home / News / Matter 1.3 Certification
Product Update January 15, 2025 5 min read

Espressif Systems' ESP32-C6 system-on-chip (SoC) has successfully obtained Matter 1.3 certification, marking a significant milestone in the evolution of smart home technology. This certification establishes the ESP32-C6 as a leading solution for building interoperable smart home devices that seamlessly work across Apple HomeKit, Google Home, Amazon Alexa, and Samsung SmartThings ecosystems.

What is Matter 1.3?

Matter 1.3 represents the latest iteration of the unified smart home standard developed by the Connectivity Standards Alliance (CSA). This standard addresses critical interoperability challenges that have historically fragmented the smart home market, allowing devices from different manufacturers to communicate seamlessly regardless of their native ecosystems.

Technical Advantages of ESP32-C6 for Matter

Beyond basic Matter compliance, the ESP32-C6 offers several technical advantages that make it an optimal choice for Matter-enabled devices:

  • RISC-V Architecture: Future-proof open-source processor architecture with growing ecosystem support
  • Dual Radio Support: Simultaneous Wi-Fi and Thread/Zigbee operation for versatile connectivity options
  • Hardware Security: Built-in secure boot, flash encryption, and hardware random number generator
  • Power Efficiency: Optimized power management for battery-powered and always-on applications
  • Comprehensive Development Tools: ESP-IDF framework with Matter SDK integration
