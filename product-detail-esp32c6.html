ESP32-C6

Next-Generation RISC-V MCU with Wi-Fi 6, Thread & Bluetooth 5

Home / Products / ESP32-C6

Product Overview

The ESP32-C6 is a highly integrated, low-power MCU with RISC-V architecture that provides Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax), Bluetooth 5 (LE), and IEEE 802.15.4 radio connectivity. It's the first ESP32 chip to support Wi-Fi 6 and is designed for versatile IoT applications requiring future-proof connectivity.

32-bit RISC-V Single Core up to 160 MHz
Wi-Fi 6 + Bluetooth 5 + Thread/Zigbee
Matter 1.3 Ready
512 KB SRAM + 4/8/16 MB Flash
