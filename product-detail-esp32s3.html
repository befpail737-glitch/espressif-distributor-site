ESP32-S3

High-Performance MCU with AI Acceleration and Advanced Connectivity

Home / Products / ESP32-S3

Product Overview

The ESP32-S3 is a highly integrated, low-power, 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi and Bluetooth 5 (LE) system-on-chip (SoC) solution that integrates advanced AI acceleration capabilities. It features a dual-core Xtensa LX7 CPU, vector instructions for AI acceleration, and a rich set of peripherals.

Dual-Core Xtensa LX7 up to 240 MHz
Wi-Fi 4 + Bluetooth 5 (LE)
AI Acceleration with Vector Instructions
Up to 8.5 MB PSRAM + 384 KB ROM
